Check the Soil

🚿
How to Water

Water thoroughly until it drains from bottom holes.

⚠️
Signs of Problems

Yellow leaves = overwatering. Wilting = underwatering.

☀️ Light Requirements

Bright Indirect Light

Place near a window but out of direct sunlight.

Best Placement
  • 1-3 feet from a south-facing window
  • Right next to an east or west window
  • Avoid dark corners
Signs & Adjustments

Pale leaves = too little light. Brown spots = too much direct sun.

🚨 Common Problems & Solutions

🟡 Yellow Leaves

Usually overwatering or poor drainage

Reduce watering frequency, check soil drainage

🟤 Brown Tips

Low humidity or fluoride in water

Use filtered water, increase humidity with pebble tray

🕷️ Pests

Spider mites, aphids, or scale insects

Spray with neem oil or insecticidal soap weekly

🌿 Propagation Guide

Stem Cutting Method
  1. Cut healthy stem with 2-3 nodes
  2. Place in water or moist soil
  3. Keep in bright, indirect light
  4. Roots appear in 2-4 weeks
Tips for Success

Change water every few days, use rooting hormone for faster results.
